# Recipe manager app

## Application idea and purpose

Recipe manager helps the user store and view their recipes. They can add new recipes, look through them and also view ideas for community. They can check their account and find the two secrets to better cooking. The purpose of the application is to help the users start their own cooking journey!

## Wikihow

1. Open the application in your browser.
2. On the **Home** page:

   * View the total number of saved recipes.
   * Browse saved recipes.
   * Click **"Add recipe"** to add a new recipe.

3. In the **Add recipe** page:

   * Enter the recipe name.
   * Enter the ingredients.
   * Click **Save** to store the recipe.

4. Use the bottom navigation bar to switch between:

   * **Home**
   * **Articles**

5. Click the **account icon** in the top right corner to open the Account page.

6. On the **Account** page:

   * View user information.
   * Click one of the secret buttons to reveal a cooking secret.
